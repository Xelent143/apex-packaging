### Poly Bag Supplier – Custom Flexible    Packaging Solution Provider

#### **Full-Service Solutions Throughout North America**

**Polynova is a comprehensive flexible packaging solution provider producing a wide range of plastic bag and films.** As a leading poly bag supplier serving the Canadian and US markets since 1981, we deliver customized, high quality print solutions for plastic bags, pouches and other packaging options. When you need a poly bag or pouch that is practical, affordable and does justice to your product and brand image, Polynova is your best choice.

**Quick turnaround** and **unbeatable quality** coupled with **excellent customer service** is why customers depend on us as their custom flexible packaging solution provider.

Headquartered in Richmond, BC, Canada, our strategic South-Western location helps us reach out and cater to the needs of our customers across Canada and the USA.

All our product lines are manufactured upon **custom specification**. Years of manufacturing have helped us master the poly bag technology to ensure that our customers have the required **flexibility** to combine the properties of different product groups. For example, a full-colour high quality print handle bag can be ordered in both, bio-degradable or reusable form. Or, a temper proof bag can be made of any type of coex, or laminated film. We can supply a very wide range of PE and PP materials from LDPE, HDPE, CPE, PPE, PP, OPP, or PVC, to non-woven cloth or biodegradable material.

The number of choices and variations are literally infinite; **you only have to tell us what you need and we’ll deliver**!
