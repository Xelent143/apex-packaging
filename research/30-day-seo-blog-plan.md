# 30-Day SEO Blog Plan — Apex Packaging Solutions

Goal: publish one practical procurement article per day for 30 days, covering Apex Packaging Solutions' full range: custom corrugated boxes, mailer boxes, polybags, industrial bulk packaging, protective packaging, design support, sustainable packaging, low MOQ orders, fast Canadian lead times, and industry-specific packaging needs.

Current status in this worktree: Days 1-15 published as of 2026-06-24.

Sources used:
- Existing Apex keyword research in `research/keywords.md`
- Existing service pages and strategy notes
- Public search review on June 9, 2026 for Canadian packaging SERPs and current sustainability/procurement themes

## Product And Offer Coverage

- Corrugated boxes: RSC, FOL, HSC, five-panel wrap, die-cut, telescoping, ECT grades, single-wall, double-wall, print options.
- Mailer boxes: tuck-top mailers, printed mailers, ecommerce packaging, small batch runs, inside/outside print, sample process.
- Polybags: LDPE, HDPE, anti-static, wicket bags, gusseted bags, garment bags, mattress bags, produce bags, poly tubing, poly sheeting, gauges.
- Industrial and bulk packaging: Gaylord boxes, pallet wrap, stretch film, shrink film, edge protectors, bulk containers, void fill.
- Protective packaging: foam inserts, bubble, air pillows, paper void fill, VCI rust-prevention, fragile goods packing.
- Sustainability: FSC-certified corrugated, recycled content, kraft packaging, compostable and PCR poly options, claims buyers can verify.
- Design support: dielines, artwork checks, 3D mockups, structural design, quote-ready specs.
- Industries: automotive, food and beverage, pharma and medical, electronics, industrial supply, ecommerce and D2C.

## Publishing Rules

- Each post targets one primary keyword and 5-10 semantic variants.
- Each post links to at least two service pages and one related blog post when available.
- Each post includes one original image or technical diagram in `public/images/blog/`.
- Each post includes Article schema through `buildArticleSchema`.
- Titles stay under roughly 60 characters when possible; meta descriptions stay near 150-160 characters.
- Copy must pass `npm run lint:slop`.

## Daily Execution Workflow

1. Pick the next unpublished row.
2. Confirm the target service page and internal links.
3. Write an 850-1,200 word article with one original image.
4. Add the article route in `src/pages/blog/`.
5. Add the post to `src/pages/blog/index.astro`.
6. Run `npm run lint:slop` and `npm run build`.
7. Commit to a dated branch named `codex/seo-blog-day-N`.
8. Push the branch to GitHub.
