# Sustainable packaging options — with paperwork that survives an ESG audit.

Procurement teams under sustainability targets need more than a "made from recycled materials" claim on a website. They need certification numbers, recycled-content percentages by SKU, ink composition disclosures, and end-of-life documentation. Apex provides all four as standard.

## FSC-certified corrugated

Apex holds FSC chain-of-custody certification on its corrugated lines. Every order can be supplied with the certification number and a stamped chain-of-custody declaration. FSC-certified product carries the FSC label on the carton if requested.

What it means: the fibre is traceable from a responsibly managed forest through the mill, the converter, and Apex, with each link audited.

## Recycled content

Recycled content varies by liner and flute:

| Product | Typical PCR content | Maximum available |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Kraft liner (single-wall) | 30 – 50% | 80% |
| Medium fluting | 70 – 100% | 100% |
| Bleached liner | 30 – 50% | 50% |
| Polybag film (LDPE/HDPE) | 0% (standard) | 30 – 50% PCR |
| Compostable polybag film | n/a (compostable, not recycled) | EN 13432 certified |

Apex's sales team will quote the PCR content for the specific product being ordered. Documentation provided on request.

## Inks and coatings

- **Soy-based and vegetable-based inks** on flexo lines. Lower VOC than petroleum-based.
- **Water-based coatings** on most retail packaging.
- **No PFAS** ("forever chemicals") in any of Apex's grease-resistant food-contact coatings — independently tested.

## Compostable polybag film

EN 13432-certified compostable polybag film for buyers with composting infrastructure. Use cases: organic food brands, garden/horticulture brands, compost-bag retail.

Important note: compostable film only composts in industrial composting facilities. It is not home-compostable. Apex will clarify the right disposal pathway for your customer base.

## Carbon-footprint disclosure

For large customers under Scope 3 reporting, Apex can provide a packaging-specific carbon footprint estimate using the European COMET methodology — kg CO₂-equivalent per 1,000 units, calculated against material, transport, and end-of-life. Available on standing customers with annual volume thresholds.

## What sustainability claims Apex will and will not make

Apex will state:
- FSC certification number and chain-of-custody status.
- PCR percentage by SKU.
- Ink composition.
- End-of-life classification (recyclable / compostable / landfill).

Apex will not state:
- "Eco-friendly" without specifying which dimension (it's a meaningless term).
- Recyclability claims without verifying local infrastructure (some films aren't recyclable in all provinces).
- Carbon-neutrality unless verified by a third party with offsets.

The reason: sustainability claims face increasing regulatory scrutiny in Canada (Competition Bureau enforcement under amended Competition Act). Greenwashing has real legal exposure. Apex's claims are conservative and documented.
