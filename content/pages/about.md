# A packaging supplier that quotes in days, not weeks.

Apex Packaging Solutions is a Canadian manufacturer of custom corrugated boxes, polybags, and protective packaging — built specifically for procurement teams who are tired of overseas lead times, oversized MOQs, and suppliers who don't return calls.

## What Apex actually does

- Manufactures custom corrugated boxes (RSC, FOL, die-cut, mailers, telescoping) in single-wall and double-wall configurations.
- Extrudes and converts LDPE, HDPE, and PCR-content polybag film, including anti-static, food-safe, and compostable grades.
- Supplies bulk packaging (Gaylord boxes, stretch and shrink film, edge protectors) to plants on blanket POs.
- Builds protective packaging stacks (custom foam inserts, void fill, VCI) specified to ISTA drop-test classes.
- Provides in-house structural design — dieline plus 3D mockup in 48–72 hours.

## What Apex won't do

- **International shipping** — Apex ships within Canada. US customers are referred to our cross-border partner.
- **Glass, metal, or rigid plastic containers** — Apex is corrugated and flexible-film only.
- **Stock-and-ship with no customization** — Apex's value is custom production. For pure stock distribution, larger national distributors are a better fit.

Naming what we don't do is faster than the buyer figuring it out mid-quote.

## Capabilities

- **Corrugated converting** — flexo, digital, and litho-laminated print. Die-cutting, scoring, slotting, gluing.
- **Polybag extrusion and conversion** — single and multi-layer film, blown and cast lines, 1.0 mil through 6.0 mil.
- **In-house structural design** — CAD-based dieline drawing, drop-test specification, ISTA pre-test.
- **Quality systems** — ISO 9001, FSC chain-of-custody, SQF on food-contact lines.

## What sets Apex apart from larger Canadian suppliers

- **Lead time**: 7–14 days from artwork sign-off. Most competitors quote 4–6 weeks.
- **MOQ**: 100 units on stock corrugated, 250 on custom-printed flexo, 500 on custom polybags. Most competitors start at 1,000–5,000.
- **Design support**: included with orders over MOQ, not invoiced separately.
- **Sustainability documentation**: FSC numbers, PCR percentages, ink composition, EN 13432 certs — provided as part of the standard delivery package, not on request only.

## How a typical engagement runs

1. **Brief** — send dimensions, MOQ, target lead time, sustainability requirements via the [contact page](/contact).
2. **Quote** — pricing returns within one business day.
3. **Design** — dieline plus 3D mockup within 48–72 hours.
4. **Sample** (optional) — physical pre-production sample 5–7 business days after design approval.
5. **Production** — 7–14 business days from artwork sign-off.
6. **Ship** — national LTL or FTL; freight arranged or coordinated.
