# Custom mailer boxes — branded outside, printed inside, shipped in 7–14 days.

For Canadian e-commerce and D2C brands that want a real unboxing without a 5,000-unit MOQ or a 6-week wait. Apex manufactures custom-printed mailer boxes domestically with MOQ from **250 units** and lead times of **7–14 days** from artwork sign-off.

## Structural options

- **Tuck-top mailer** — friction-tab closure, the standard e-commerce mailer.
- **Magnetic-close mailer** — premium retail-feel, hidden magnet closure.
- **ZIP-tear opening** — perforated pull-strip for unboxing experience.
- **Dispenser-style** — open-flap design for repeat-access products.
- **Custom dieline** — Apex's design team will draw a structural dieline from your product dimensions.

## Print options

- **Exterior full-colour print** with one-pass digital or flexo. Spot colour matching to brand swatch.
- **Interior print** — one colour or full-colour. Used for inside-lid messaging or pattern.
- **Spot UV, foil stamp, embossing** — available on litho-laminated stock.
- **Spot coatings** — matte, soft-touch, gloss.

## Material options

- E-flute corrugated (standard for mailers) — light, printable, structurally rigid.
- B-flute (heavier feel, more protection).
- FSC-certified liner on request.
- 30–100% recycled content available.

## Sample process

1. Send brand assets (logo, brand colours, any reference photos).
2. Apex returns a structural dieline and a 3D mockup in 48–72 hours.
3. Approve digitally, or order a physical pre-production sample.
4. On approval, full production runs 7–14 days.

## FAQ

**What's the minimum order on custom-printed mailers?**
250 units for flexo. 50 units for digital. Volume pricing kicks in at 1,000.

**What artwork files do you need?**
Vector logos (AI, EPS, or PDF). Brand colours as Pantone codes. Apex's design team can convert raster art to print-ready vector — included with order.

**How does the price compare to overseas suppliers?**
For runs under 5,000 units, Apex is typically cost-comparable or cheaper after freight and duty. Above 10,000 units, overseas can win on unit cost but loses 4–6 weeks of lead time and incurs higher tariff risk.

**Can I print on the inside of the box?**
Yes — one-colour or full-colour interior. Adds about 8–12% to per-unit cost depending on coverage.
