# Custom polybags manufactured in Canada — every bag type, every gauge, every print option.

For Canadian shippers, garment manufacturers, food producers, electronics assemblers, and industrial buyers. Apex extrudes and converts LDPE, HDPE, and PCR-content film domestically. MOQ from **500 units** on custom-print. Standard lead time **7–14 days**.

## Bag types

- **Flat polybags** — the workhorse for general shipping and product packaging.
- **Gusseted polybags** — side gussets expand to hold bulkier items (apparel, dry goods, foam).
- **Wicket bags** — pre-loaded on a wicket for high-speed bagging lines (bakery, produce, automotive).
- **Coreless bags** — folded on rolls, dispenser-friendly for retail and back-of-house.
- **Slider and ZIP bags** — reclosable, for resealable consumer products.
- **Pre-opened bags on rolls (POR)** — auto-bagger compatible.

## Vertical-specific specs

- **Garment bags** — clear or printed, low-density, with vent holes if required.
- **Mattress bags** — heavy-gauge (4–6 mil), gusseted, capable of 14"+ depth.
- **Furniture bags** — extra-large, reinforced seam, dust-protection grade.
- **Produce bags** — vented, FDA-compliant film, often wicket-loaded.
- **Pound bags** — pre-cut, suitable for produce and bakery weighing.
- **PTO bags** — pull-tab opening for retail dispensing.

## FAQ

**What gauge do I need?**
- Light retail / garment: 1.0–2.0 mil.
- General shipping: 2.0–4.0 mil.
- Heavy industrial / furniture / mattress: 4.0–6.0 mil.
Apex's team will recommend a gauge based on your product weight, edge sharpness, and stacking pattern. See the [poly bag thickness guide](/blog/poly-bag-thickness-guide) for details.

**Are food-grade polybags certified?**
Yes — Apex's food-contact film carries FDA-compliant resin certification and Health Canada acceptance. Documentation provided with every order.

**Can polybags be recycled in Canada?**
LDPE and HDPE bags are recyclable at film drop-off programs across most Canadian municipalities. Apex offers PCR-content film (30–50% post-consumer recycled) and compostable film for buyers with stricter requirements. See [Sustainability](/sustainability).

**What's the MOQ on custom-print?**
500 units on 1-color flexo. 1,000 on multi-color. Volume pricing at 10,000+.
