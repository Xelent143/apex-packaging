# Send your packaging spec. Get a quote within one business day.

For the fastest reply, include:

1. **Product type** — what you're packaging (a brief description and rough dimensions).
2. **Packaging type needed** — corrugated box, mailer, polybag, foam insert, bulk container, etc. If unsure, the design team will recommend.
3. **Target MOQ** — how many units you actually want to order.
4. **Lead time needed** — when you need it delivered.
5. **Sustainability requirements**, if any — FSC, PCR minimum, compostable, etc.
6. **Brand assets** — logo and brand colours if it's a printed product. Vector preferred (AI/EPS/PDF).
7. **Existing spec or sample**, if you're transferring from another supplier.

The procurement team responds within one business day with pricing, a proposed structural spec, and a lead-time commitment. Design work (dieline + 3D mockup) follows within 48–72 hours if you proceed.
