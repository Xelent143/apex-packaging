# Six service lines. One Canadian manufacturing footprint.

Apex manufactures custom corrugated boxes, mailer boxes, polybags, industrial and bulk packaging, and protective packaging — and includes structural design support on every order over MOQ. Each service below has its own page with specs, MOQs, lead times, and a quote form.

## Corrugated boxes
RSC, HSC, FOL, five-panel wrap, die-cut, telescoping. Single-wall (ECT-32) and double-wall (ECT-44 / ECT-48). MOQ from 100 units on stock styles, 250 on custom-printed.

## Mailer boxes
Custom-printed shipping boxes for D2C and e-commerce. Tuck-top, magnetic close, ZIP-tear opening, dispenser-style. Full-color exterior; printed interior optional. MOQ from 250 units.

## Polybags
LDPE, HDPE, and PCR-content film. Flat, gusseted, wicket, coreless, slider, ZIP. Gauges from 1.0 mil to 6.0 mil. Anti-static (pink), FDA-compliant, and compostable options. MOQ from 500 units.

## Industrial and bulk packaging
Gaylord boxes, custom pallet wrap, stretch film (machine and hand grades), shrink film, edge protectors, corner boards. For manufacturers and distributors moving pallet quantities.

## Protective packaging
Custom-cut foam inserts (PE, PU, ESD), Instapak®, bubble wrap, paper void fill, air pillows, VCI rust-prevention paper. Specified to protection level and product weight.

## Design support
Send a logo and product dimensions. Apex's structural design team returns a print-ready dieline plus a 3D mockup within 48–72 hours. Free with any order over MOQ; flat fee for design-only engagements.
