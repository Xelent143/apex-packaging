# Custom packaging, made in Canada, shipped in 7–14 days.

Most Canadian box suppliers quote four to six weeks. Apex prints, dies, and ships custom corrugated boxes and polybags in **7–14 days from artwork sign-off** — domestic production, no overseas freight, no excuses for missing your launch.

MOQs start at **100 units** on stock RSC boxes and **250 units** on custom-printed mailers. FSC-certified liner, recycled content, soy-based inks, and compostable polybag film are available on most products. In-house design team turns a logo into a print-ready dieline and a 3D mockup within 48–72 hours.

## What we manufacture

- **Corrugated boxes** — RSC, FOL, die-cut, telescoping, five-panel wrap, full-overlap. Single-wall and double-wall, ECT-32 through ECT-44.
- **Mailer boxes** — tuck-top, magnetic close, ZIP-tear, dispenser. Full-color print outside; one-color or full-color inside.
- **Polybags** — flat, gusseted, wicket, coreless, slider, ZIP. LDPE and HDPE, 1.0 mil through 6.0 mil. Anti-static and FDA / food-safe options.
- **Industrial & bulk packaging** — Gaylord boxes, custom pallet wrap, stretch and shrink film, edge protectors.
- **Protective packaging** — custom-cut foam inserts, Instapak®, bubble wrap, paper void fill, VCI rust-prevention.
