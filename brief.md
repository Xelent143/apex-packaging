# Apex Packaging Solutions — Project Brief

Locked: 2026-06-02 · Project: full site rebuild (Astro + Tailwind + TS)

## 1. Business identity
- **Name**: Apex Packaging Solutions
- **Industry**: Custom packaging manufacturer
- **Product range**: Corrugated boxes (RSC, mailer, die-cut, telescoping), polybags (LDPE, HDPE, gusseted, custom-print), foam inserts, void fill, custom labels, shrink wrap, stretch film.
- **Existing URL**: apexpackagingsolutions.com (treated as out-of-scope reference — building clean from research)
- **One-line description**: A Canadian custom packaging manufacturer producing corrugated boxes and polybags for industrial and B2B manufacturers — with low MOQs, 7–14-day turnaround, in-house design support, and FSC / recycled material options.

## 2. Geography
- **Operating base**: Canada (national)
- **Service radius**: Ships across all provinces
- **SEO posture**: National Canadian SEO — keyword targets "custom packaging Canada", "corrugated boxes Canada", "polybags Canada", "custom box manufacturer Canada". Head-office city mentioned in footer / contact but not as a local-SEO anchor.

## 3. Target audience (primary)
**Industrial / B2B manufacturers needing bulk packaging.**

- Verticals: auto parts, electronics, food production, pharmaceutical, industrial supply.
- Order pattern: pallet quantities, repeat orders, often quarterly POs.
- Decision criteria: protection, cost per unit, lead times, certifications (FSC, food-safe, ISO), supplier reliability.
- Decision-makers: procurement managers, plant managers, ops directors. Not founders / marketers.
- Pain points the buyer has today: long lead times from overseas / large suppliers, MOQs locked at 5,000+ units, no proactive packaging design help, sustainability requirements but no compliant Canadian supplier they trust.

## 4. Differentiators (USPs — all four are real and stack)
1. **Fast turnaround**: 7–14 days vs industry standard 4–6 weeks. Domestic Canadian production.
2. **Low MOQs**: small-batch friendly (100–500 unit runs possible) — useful when buyers test new SKUs or run promo batches.
3. **Sustainable materials**: FSC-certified corrugated, recycled liner, kraft, compostable polybag options, soy-based inks.
4. **Full design support**: in-house design team handles dieline + structural design + print prep + 3D mockups. Buyer provides logo; Apex delivers ready-to-print artwork.
