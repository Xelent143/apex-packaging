## LinkedIn Post

Retail display packaging quotes usually get weak when the team sends artwork before it sends the merchandising conditions.

For counter trays, shelf displays, and PDQs, a stronger brief usually answers:

1. What is the loaded weight, not just the product weight?
2. What shelf, counter, or pallet footprint has to be respected?
3. Which panels stay visible after the display is stocked?
4. Does the unit ship flat, assembled empty, or pre-loaded?
5. Does the program need a sample to confirm strength, opening behavior, and replenishment flow?

That matters because a retail display box is not just a printed carton.

The structure has to hold product weight.
The artwork has to stay visible in store.
The replenishment method has to work for retail staff, not just the brand mockup.
