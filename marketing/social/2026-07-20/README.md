## LinkedIn Post

CBD packaging quotes usually break down at the same point: the team sends artwork direction before it sends the real packaging constraints.

For cartons, sleeves, rigid boxes, and display-ready CBD programs, a stronger brief usually answers:

1. What product is going in the pack, and what are the exact dimensions?
2. Which panels need room for client-supplied warnings, barcode zones, and batch details?
3. Does the format need a child-resistant opening logic or tamper-evident feature?
4. Is the package built for shelf display, ecommerce shipment, launch kits, or a multi-SKU retail line?
5. Does the team need a sample to confirm fit, finish, and opening behavior before production?

That matters because a lower first quote does not help if the structure changes after artwork review.

Material can change.
Insert fit can change.
Closure style can change.
Lead time can change.
