## LinkedIn Post

Large polybag quotes usually drift for one reason: the buyer sends nominal product size instead of the packed product spec.

For mattress bags and gusseted polybags, that difference matters immediately.

Before requesting pricing, send:

1. Finished length, width, and maximum packed thickness
2. Packed weight
3. Bag style: flat, side gusseted, bottom gusseted, or open to recommendation
4. Loading method: hand load, repeat pack line, compression, fold, or roll
5. Closure, vent, warning print, or label-area requirements
6. First-run quantity, reorder volume, and delivery location

That is what helps a supplier recommend the right gauge, seam, and clearance before the first sample gets blamed for a measurement problem.

The latest Apex article walks through how mattress bags and other large gusseted polybags should be measured before quoting.
