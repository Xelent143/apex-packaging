## LinkedIn Post

Fast-turnaround packaging rarely slips because of production first.

The schedule usually breaks earlier:

1. The RFQ is missing dimensions, quantity, delivery location, or a real in-hand date
2. Artwork is not built to the dieline
3. Proofs move through several reviewers in sequence
4. The structure is more complex than the deadline allows

For a cleaner rush quote, buyers should send:

- finished dimensions
- packed weight
- quantity
- artwork status
- delivery location
- required in-hand date
- one proof approver

That gives the supplier room to recommend the fastest practical route, whether that is a straightforward corrugated carton, a printed mailer, or a quick design-support step before production starts.
