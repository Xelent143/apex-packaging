## LinkedIn Post

Custom kraft box quotes usually go sideways when the conversation starts with look and price before the product and route are defined.

For natural-product brands, a stronger brief usually answers:

1. Is the pack for retail shelf, ecommerce parcel, gift presentation, or a multi-channel program?
2. Does the brand want a warmer kraft tone, a lighter recycled look, or a recommendation based on print contrast?
3. Is the artwork ready, or does the dieline still need design support?
4. Will samples be used to confirm fit, closure, and scuff resistance?
5. What quantity, timing, and reorder cadence should the quote reflect?

That matters because kraft packaging is not one generic material choice.

Board tone changes how black, white, and spot colours read.
Structure changes how the product is packed and protected.
Sampling changes how much risk remains before production.
