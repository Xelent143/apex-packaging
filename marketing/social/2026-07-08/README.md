## LinkedIn Post

The cheapest packaging quote is often the least complete one.

When procurement teams compare suppliers, the line-item price hides the real operational questions:

1. When does the quoted lead time actually start
2. What MOQ applies to this size, SKU, and print method
3. What documentation supports the material or sustainability claim
4. Which sample level is included and what does it add to timing
5. What assumptions are still missing before the quote is production-ready

That is why two quotes that look similar can perform very differently once artwork, samples, approvals, and freight are involved.

The latest Apex guide gives buyers a practical packaging supplier checklist for Canada, focused on lead time, MOQ, certifications, samples, and quote quality.

It is also a useful internal tool when you need to defend a supplier decision beyond unit cost alone.
