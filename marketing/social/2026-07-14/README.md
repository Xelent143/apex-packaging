## LinkedIn Post

Custom mailer box quotes get messy when the team compares unit price before defining the shipment.

Subscription packaging usually needs a few decisions locked early:

1. What product mix is going in each shipment
2. Whether the route is parcel, courier, or pallet replenishment
3. How much presentation matters versus scuff resistance
4. Whether inserts, tissue, cards, or sample sachets change the fit
5. If artwork is ready or the dieline still needs design support

That is why a "quote me a branded mailer" brief is often too thin.

A stronger RFQ names:

- mailer dimensions or product dimensions
- packed weight
- board preference such as kraft, white, E-flute, or recommendation
- print coverage outside and inside
- insert need and sample requirement
- order quantity, launch date, and reorder expectation
- delivery destination and route
