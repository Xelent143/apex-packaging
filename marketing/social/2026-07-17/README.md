## LinkedIn Post

Poly mailer quotes for apparel usually get more expensive when the team starts with artwork before it starts with the shipment.

For apparel and soft-goods programs, a stronger brief usually answers:

1. What are the folded dimensions and packed weight?
2. Does the bag need full opacity, partial opacity, or a clear window?
3. Is the route parcel, courier, returns-heavy ecommerce, or retail replenishment?
4. Does the closure need a single strip, double strip for returns, or a different sealing method?
5. Is the job standard LDPE, lighter-gauge film, PCR content, or a sustainability-led spec that needs documentation?

That matters because a poly mailer is not one generic line item.

Film gauge changes puncture resistance and cost.
Opacity changes privacy and brand presentation.
Closure choice changes the customer returns experience.
